Mountain trip

Running to mountains is what I do when I want to get out of city for a short time.

Like last week I decided to run to Butakovka for the first time. I ran more than half of the way up and walked by step remaining distance to the waterfall.
I have had a great time over there even though I was alone.

After taking pictures and walking even further than the waterfall I decided to go back because
it was getting late. Then I ran all the way down. It was cool, funny and hard at the same time. In some time I will get back to reach another waterfall over there.

Actually I rarely go to mountains but I do my best once I go there.
This winter I climbed one mountain near Medeo and I did my best to make it happen. So till next time!
